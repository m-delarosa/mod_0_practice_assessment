Making a Sandwich

1. Gather Materials
  1.1 Grab items from pantry
    1.1.1 Grab sliced bread
      1.1.1.1 Locate sliced bread's position
        1.1.1.2 Scan pantry with eyes left to right, top down until you locate sliced bread
      1.1.1.2 Extend arm into pantry towards location of sliced bread
        1.1.1.2.1 Position right arm with your elbow in a 90 degree angle
        1.1.1.2.1 Elongate right arm by pushing your hand away from your body, towards the location of sliced bread until your elbow reaches 180 degrees
      1.1.1.3 Grasp sliced bread in hands
        1.1.1.3.1 Position sliced bread between your fingers and thumb
        1.1.1.3.2 Move thumb and fingers together until sliced bread is under pressure of all 5 digits
      1.1.1.4 Return arm towards body
        1.1.1.4.1 Return your arm to a 90 degree position at the elbow
    1.1.2 Grab peanut butter
      1.1.2.1 Locate peanut butter's position
        1.1.2.1.1 Scan pantry with eyes left to right, top down until you locate peanut butter
      1.1.2.2 Extend arm into pantry towards location of peanut butter
        1.1.2.2.1 Position left arm with your elbow in a 90 degree angle
        1.1.2.2.2 Elongate left arm by pushing your hand away from your body, towards the location of peanut butter until your elbow reaches 180 degrees
      1.1.2.3 Grasp sliced bread in hands
        1.1.2.3.1 Position sliced bread between your fingers and thumb
        1.1.2.3.2 Move thumb and fingers together until sliced bread is under pressure of all 5 digits
      1.1.2.4 Return left arm towards body
        1.1.2.4.1 Return your left arm to a 90 degree position at the elbow
    1.1.3 Close pantry door
      1.1.3.1 Position yourself adjacent to pantry door
        1.1.3.1.1 Walk towards open end of pantry door
        1.1.3.1.2 Rotate hips clockwise until the line up with pantry door
        1.1.3.1.3 Allow your feet to rotate in the same direction of your hips until they are directly back under your hips
      1.1.3.2 Place elbow on pantry door edge
        1.1.3.2.1 Identify arm closest to pantry door
        1.1.3.2.2 Left arm closest to pantry door until shoulder and side reach 90 degree angle
        1.1.3.2.3 Rotate torso until raised elbow meets outside face of pantry door
        1.1.3.2.4 Continue rotating torso in same direction until pantry door can no longer move
